Introducing Sign-Lish, Our website is designed to help you master the basics of American Sign Language (ASL), starting with the alphabet!

With Sign-Lish, you'll learn how to express each letter using clear and interactive tutorials. It's the perfect starting point for anyone interested in communicating through ASL.

J M A

The name Sign - Lish combines "Sign", meaning signal or sign language, and "Lish" from English. It reflects our website's goal to teach ASL basics while connecting sign language with English communication.

Benefits of Sign - Lish

Easy Learning :

Step-by-step guides and visual demonstrations make learning ASL alphabet simple and fun.

Interactive Practice :

Engage in activities to test and refine your skills, ensuring you're mastering the hand shapes for each letter.

Once you've mastered the ASL alphabet, you can :

  • Fingerspell Names and Words :

    Start spelling out names and common words for basic communication.

  • Advance to Full ASL :

    After getting the alphabet down, expand into more complex phrases and full conversations in ASL.

  • Connect with the Deaf Community :

    Learning ASL allows you to engage more meaningfully with the Deaf community and break down communication barriers.

With Sign - Lish, you're taking the first step toward
becoming fluent in ASL !
